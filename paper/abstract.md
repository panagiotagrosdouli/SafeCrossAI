# Abstract

SafeCrossAI is a research software scaffold for intelligent-intersection vulnerable road-user trajectory prediction, social-interaction modelling, and interpretable risk estimation. The current repository implements deterministic synthetic trajectory generation, a constant-velocity forecasting baseline, trajectory metrics, social-agent and scene abstractions, neighbor search, interaction graph construction, time-to-interaction utilities, and baseline risk scoring. The platform is structured to support future public-dataset evaluation, learning-based predictors, uncertainty calibration, and intelligent-intersection simulation. No benchmark performance is claimed at this stage; the repository provides the reproducible foundation required for future empirical work.
