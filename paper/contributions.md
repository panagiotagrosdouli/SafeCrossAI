# Contributions

SafeCrossAI should be presented as a developing research platform, not as a completed benchmark paper.

## Current Contributions

1. A modular software scaffold for intelligent-intersection trajectory prediction and interaction analysis.
2. A deterministic constant-velocity baseline for reproducible smoke tests.
3. Agent, scene, sequence, and interaction-graph abstractions for multi-agent traffic scenes.
4. Baseline time-to-interaction and closest-approach utilities.
5. Interpretable pairwise risk reports built from geometric interaction features.
6. Documentation that separates implemented, prototype, and planned components.

## Not Yet Contributions

The following are planned research directions and must not be described as completed contributions:

- neural trajectory prediction;
- public dataset benchmark results;
- infrastructure perception;
- calibrated uncertainty estimation;
- CARLA/SUMO digital-twin simulation;
- deployment-ready decision support.

## Future Contribution Targets

- Interaction-aware trajectory prediction on public intersection datasets.
- Calibration-aware risk estimation for predicted future trajectories.
- Benchmark comparison against non-interaction-aware baselines.
- Scientific visualizations suitable for conference papers.
